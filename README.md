# ⚡ EV KPI Analysis: Automotive Business Intelligence (Tableau)

## 👋 Overview

The electric vehicle (EV) industry is rapidly evolving, requiring continuous monitoring of Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) to evaluate market adoption and operational efficiency. This project utilizes **Tableau** to analyze a comprehensive EV dataset, transforming raw performance metrics into an interactive executive dashboard that supports data-driven strategic planning.

## 🚀 Key Features & Analysis

  * **Sales Performance Tracking:** Visualized total EV sales and identified year-over-year growth patterns.
  * **Efficiency Benchmarking:** Analyzed battery capacity and energy efficiency metrics across different vehicle models.
  * **Infrastructure Assessment:** Evaluated charging infrastructure availability and its correlation with regional EV adoption.
  * **Temporal Trend Analysis:** Identified seasonal variations in EV performance and market behavior.
  * **Comparative KPI Analysis:** Built side-by-side comparisons of high-performing vs. low-performing metrics to pinpoint operational bottlenecks.

## 🛠️ Technical Toolkit

  * **Core Tool:** **Tableau Desktop**
  * **Advanced Features:** Utilized **Calculated Fields** for derived KPIs (Growth Rate, Average Efficiency).
  * **Data Architecture:** Applied **Data Blending** and complex **Filtering** to handle multi-dimensional categories.
  * **Dashboard Design:** Implemented KPI cards, interactive line charts for trends, and dynamic bar charts for segment comparisons.

## ⚙️ How It Works

1.  **Phase 1: Data Preparation** – Removed duplicate records, standardized KPI units (e.g., kWh for battery capacity), and handled missing values.
2.  **Phase 2: Data Modeling** – Structured a logical data hierarchy in Tableau to support seamless aggregation across time and vehicle categories.
3.  **Phase 3: Calculation Logic** – Created custom formulas to calculate **Total EV Sales**, **Average Efficiency Metrics**, and **KPI Growth Rates**.
4.  **Phase 4: Dashboard Visualization** – Designed an interactive interface allowing users to filter data by time period and vehicle category for deep-dive analysis.

## 📈 Key Business Insights

  * **Exponential Growth:** Identified clear upward trends in EV adoption, validating the global shift toward sustainable transportation.
  * **Efficiency Leaders:** Isolated specific battery configurations that deliver the highest energy-to-range efficiency.
  * **Seasonal Variance:** Data revealed measurable performance fluctuations in EVs during different seasons, suggesting areas for technical optimization.
  * **Infrastructure Gap:** High-performing sales regions were directly correlated with high charging station density, highlighting infrastructure as a primary sales driver.
